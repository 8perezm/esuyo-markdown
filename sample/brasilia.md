# 🏗️ Brasília, Brazil

**Population:** 3.1 million (metro)  
**Language:** Portuguese  
**Currency:** Brazilian Real (R$)

---

## 🌇 About Brasília

Brasília is the capital of Brazil, inaugurated on **April 21, 1960**. It was a revolutionary experiment — a **completely planned city** designed in the shape of an aeroplane or bird, built in just 41 months.

> "Brasília is a landmark in the history of town planning." — *UNESCO*

## 🏛️ Architectural Masterpieces

| Landmark | Architect | Why it's special |
|----------|-----------|-----------------|
| Cathedral of Brasília | Oscar Niemeyer | Hyperboloid structure, 16 concrete columns |
| National Congress | Oscar Niemeyer | Twin towers, two hemispherical domes |
| Palácio da Alvorada | Oscar Niemeyer | Presidential residence, glass facade |
| JK Memorial | Oscar Niemeyer | Houses President Kubitschek's remains |
| Supreme Federal Court | Oscar Niemeyer | Modernist sculpture-like building |

## ✈️ The City Layout

Brasília's urban plan (the *Plano Piloto*) is shaped like an aeroplane:

- **Fuselage** (Monumental Axis) — Government buildings
- **Wings** (North & South) — Residential superblocks
  - Each block has its own school, church, shops, and green areas

## 📅 Quick Facts

1. Brasília was built in **1,300 days** — a world record
2. It's the **only 20th-century city** designated a UNESCO World Heritage Site
3. The city has **no traffic lights** in the monumental axis (all pedestrian crossings are underground)
4. The *Cerrado* (Brazilian savanna) is one of the world's most biodiverse biomes
5. Brasília was designed for **500,000 people**; today it's home to over 3 million
