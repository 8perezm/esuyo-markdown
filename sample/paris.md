# 🥐 Paris, France

**Population:** 2.1 million (metro: 12 million)  
**Language:** French  
**Currency:** Euro (€)

---

## 💛 About Paris

Paris, the *City of Light*, is the capital of France. Known for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and romantic ambience, it's one of the most visited cities on Earth.

> "Paris is always a good idea." — *Audrey Hepburn*

## 🏛️ Must-See Landmarks

| Landmark | Built | Notes |
|----------|-------|-------|
| Eiffel Tower | 1889 | 330 m, originally meant to be temporary |
| Louvre Museum | 1793 | *Mona Lisa* lives here |
| Notre-Dame | 1345 | Gothic cathedral (reopened 2024) |
| Sacré-Cœur | 1914 | White-domed basilica on Montmartre |
| Arc de Triomphe | 1836 | Commands 12 avenues |

## 🥘 French Cuisine

- **Croissant** — Flaky, buttery pastry (perfect at breakfast)
- **Coq au Vin** — Chicken braised in red wine
- **Bouillabaisse** — Traditional fish stew from Marseille
- **Crème Brûlée** — Caramelised custard dessert
- **Baguette** — The quintessential French bread

## 📅 Quick Facts

1. Paris has **20 arrondissements** (districts), spiralling clockwise
2. The Metro has **308 stations**
3. The Louvre is the **world's largest museum** (72,735 m²)
4. Paris was founded around **250 BC** by the Parisii tribe
5. There are **~470 parks and gardens** in the city
