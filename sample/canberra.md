## 🌄 About Canberra

Canberra is the capital of Australia, chosen in 1908 as a compromise between rivals Sydney and Melbourne. It's a **planned city** — designed by American architect Walter Burley Griffin.

> "Canberra is a city designed for the future." — *Walter Burley Griffin*

## 🏛️ Must-See Landmarks

| Landmark | Completed | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| Parliament House | 1988 | Built into Capital Hill (grass roof) |
| Australian War Memorial | 1941 | National shrine & museum |
| National Gallery | 1982 | Houses 170,000+ artworks |
| Lake Burley Griffin | 1964 | Artificial lake in the city centre |
| Black Mountain Tower | 1980 | 195 m telecommunications tower |

## 🦘 Native Australian Animals

- **Kangaroo** 🦘 — Can hop at 70 km/h
- **Koala** 🐨 — Sleeps 20 hours a day
- **Wombat** — Cube-shaped droppings!
- **Echidna** — Spiny monotreme (lays eggs)
- **Platypus** — Venomous, egg-laying mammal

## 🌳 Unique to Canberra

1. Australia's **only entirely planned city**
2. **99% renewable electricity** in the ACT region
3. Over **400 parks and nature reserves**
4. The city sits on the traditional land of the **Ngunnawal people**
5. *Fyshwick* is the only suburb with a separate light-industrial zone
