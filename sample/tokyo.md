# 🗼 Tokyo, Japan

**Population:** 14 million (metro: 37 million)  
**Language:** Japanese  
**Currency:** Japanese Yen (¥)

---

## 🌸 About Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital of Japan and the world's most populous metropolitan area. It blends ultramodern skyscrapers with historic temples and **world-class cuisine**.

> "Tokyo is not a city. It's a galaxy." — *Ryu Murakami*

## 🏛️ Must-See Landmarks

- **Senso-ji** — Tokyo's oldest temple (built 645 AD)
   - Located in Asakusa
   - Famous for the *Kaminarimon* (Thunder Gate)
- **Shibuya Crossing** — The world's busiest pedestrian crossing
- **Tokyo Tower** — 333 m, inspired by the Eiffel Tower
- **Meiji Shrine** — A Shinto shrine surrounded by forest

## 📅 Quick Facts

1. Tokyo was originally called **Edo**
2. It became the capital in **1868**
3. Hosted the **1964** and **2020** Summer Olympics
4. Has **23 special wards**, each functioning as a city
5. The Tsukiji fish market handles over **2,000 tons** of seafood daily
