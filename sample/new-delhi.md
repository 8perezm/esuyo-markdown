# 🕌 New Delhi, India

**Population:** 320,000 (Delhi metro: 33 million)  
**Languages:** Hindi, English, Punjabi, Urdu  
**Currency:** Indian Rupee (₹)

---

## 🏛️ About New Delhi

New Delhi is the capital of India, designed by British architects Edwin Lutyens and Herbert Baker. It's the **seat of all three branches of the Indian government**, built as a grand imperial capital — now the heart of the world's largest democracy.

> "Delhi is not just a city; it's a living museum of history." — *Khushwant Singh*

## 🏛️ Must-See Landmarks

- **Red Fort** (Lal Qila) — Mughal fortress built 1639–1648
   - Site of the Prime Minister's Independence Day address
- **India Gate** — 42 m war memorial (built 1931)
- **Qutb Minar** — 73 m victory tower (built 1193) — UNESCO site
- **Lotus Temple** — Baháʼí House of Worship, shaped like a lotus flower
- **Humayun's Tomb** — UNESCO site, precursor to the Taj Mahal

## 🍛 Indian Cuisine

A taste of India's regional diversity:

1. **North Indian** 🫓 — Butter chicken, naan, biryani
2. **South Indian** 🥥 — Dosa, idli, sambar
3. **Mughlai** 🍛 — Rich, creamy curries (korma, nihari)
4. **Street Food** 🛵 — Chaat, samosa, golgappa, jalebi
5. **Lassi** 🥤 — Yoghurt-based drink (sweet or salted)

## 🚂 Delhi Metro

> The Delhi Metro is the **12th longest** in the world (392 km, 286 stations). It carries over **5 million passengers daily**. All stations have Wi-Fi, platform screen doors, and no honking zones!

## 📅 Quick Facts

1. Delhi has been continuously inhabited since **6th century BC**
2. Seven major empires have ruled from Delhi
3. New Delhi was designed with **hexagonal, radial street patterns**
4. The city has **three seasons**: hot, hotter, and cool
5. *Chandni Chowk* (Old Delhi) has been a market since the **17th century**
