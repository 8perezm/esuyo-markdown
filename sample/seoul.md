# 🇰🇷 Seoul, South Korea

**Population:** 9.7 million (metro: 25 million)  
**Language:** Korean  
**Currency:** South Korean Won (₩)

---

## 🏙️ About Seoul

Seoul is the capital of South Korea — a **hyper-modern megacity** where ancient palaces stand alongside neon-lit skyscrapers, and K-pop beats echo through historic alleyways. It's been the capital of Korea for over **600 years**.

> "Seoul is a city that never sleeps, but always evolves." — *Popular saying*

## 🏯 Palaces of Seoul (The Big Five)

1. **Gyeongbokgung** — Main palace of the Joseon dynasty
2. **Changdeokgung** — UNESCO World Heritage (beautiful *secret garden*)
3. **Changgyeonggung** — Adjoined to Changdeokgung
4. **Deoksugung** — Blends traditional & Western architecture
5. **Gyeonghuigung** — Secondary palace, smaller but serene

2. The subway system carries **7+ million passengers daily**
3. Gangnam district was made famous by PSY's *"Gangnam Style"*
4. Seoul is **carbon-neutral** by 2050 target
5. There are **over 25,000 coffee shops** in the city
