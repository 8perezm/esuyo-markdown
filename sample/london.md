# 🏰 London, United Kingdom

**Population:** 8.9 million (metro: 14 million)  
**Language:** English  
**Currency:** Pound Sterling (£)

---

## 🎡 About London

London is the capital of the United Kingdom and one of the world's most influential cities — a global hub for finance, culture, and history spanning **two millennia**.

> "When a man is tired of London, he is tired of life." — *Samuel Johnson*

## 🏛️ Must-See Landmarks

- **Big Ben** (Elizabeth Tower) — Completed 1859, 96 m tall
- **Tower of London** — Historic castle, home of the Crown Jewels
- **Buckingham Palace** — Royal residence since 1837
   - Changing of the Guard: daily at 11:00
- **London Eye** — 135 m observation wheel on the South Bank
- **Tower Bridge** — Victorian bascule bridge (built 1894)

## 📅 Quick Facts

1. The London Underground (the "Tube") opened in **1863** — world's first metro
2. London has **four UNESCO World Heritage Sites**
3. Over **300 languages** are spoken in London schools
4. The City of London (the "Square Mile") has its own government and police
5. The original London Bridge is now in **Arizona, USA**
